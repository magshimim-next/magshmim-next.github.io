Magshimim Next

The community for the alumni and alumnae of "Magshimim", an Israeli Cyber Education Center's program.

Our Target

At Magshimim Next, Our mission is to offer a community to the graduates of the Magshimim program, and help them in their day to day life and struggles, mainly work, career and education.

Magshimim

The National Cyber Program has been operating since 2010 in the geographic and social periphery of Israel as the "National Cyber Program", with the aim of encouraging curious and ambitious youth to learn in-depth professional content in the fields of computers and cyber.

The Community

At Magshimim Next, Our mission is to offer a community to the graduates of the Magshimim program, and help them in their day to day life and struggles, mainly work, career and education.
