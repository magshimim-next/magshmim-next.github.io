Magshimim Next

Magshimim-Next is the community for the alumni and alumnae of "Magshimim", an Israeli Cyber Education Center's program. "Magshimim" has been operating since 2010 in the geographic and social periphery of lsrael as the "National Cyber Program", with the aim of encouraging curious and ambitious youth to learn in-depth professional content in the fields of computers and cyber.
