Magshimim Next

A community for alumni of "Magshimim", the Israeli Cyber Education Center's program.

Our Target

At Magshimim Next, our mission is to build a supportive community for graduates of the Magshimim program, helping them navigate everyday challenges, especially in areas like work, career development, and education.

Magshimim

Launched in 2010, Magshimim was designated as the National Cyber Program, and operates across Israel's social and geographic periphery, encouraging driven young people to pursue advanced studies in computer science and cybersecurity.

The Community

Magshimim Next is made up of individuals from across Israel, representing a wide range of backgrounds and experiences, including soldiers and veterans of technological units, startup founders, academic professionals, and top-tier developers and researchers.

Community Statistics

Magshimim Next is a vibrant and active community. From engaging with and celebrating our members, to hosting events and workshops, we are always looking for new ways to connect, support, and grow together.

Our Members

Our community is made up of exceptional individuals who are leading the way in tech, cybersecurity, and innovation. From senior engineers at top companies to successful startup founders, our members are shaping the future of technology.
